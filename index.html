Welcome to Grupify!

Grupify is a tool for helping people share their contact information and stay connected with each other.

How it works:

  • Create a group on the Grupify app
  • Invite other members to join your group
  • Share your contact information with the group
  • Stay connected with other members through the app and other messaging platforms

Download the app:

To download the Grupify app, visit the Google Play Store or the App Store.
